One click to autofill all your job forms.

This is a UI demo only. In production, this button would open the official DigiLocker consent flow, and your backend would fetch documents securely after user approval.

Step 1: User consent

User clicks “Connect DigiLocker” and is redirected to the official DigiLocker screen to log in and approve access.

Step 2: Fetch documents

Your backend receives a code/token and securely fetches profile and document metadata via DigiLocker APIs.

Step 3: Autofill forms

Mapped fields (name, DOB, address, IDs) are auto-filled into job or KYC forms, ready for user confirmation.

Demo only. For real integration you must be an approved DigiLocker Requester and follow all official guidelines.
